About RAS

At ReallyAdequateSoftware LLC, we are dedicated to bringing to the public software that just works. It does what is advertised (hopefully) and doesn't do what isn't advertised. 

The journey of RAS LLC started in the basement of 18th Avenue Library at The Ohio State University. To Evan Huang and Michael Bodnar, this place is a second home. One average night, the two had the brilliant idea of developing an app. However, anyone who has had this idea knows that the hardest part is getting an original idea. After brainstorming for a while they came up with something that is now titled Recyclone. This is a game that will be released for iOS, eventually, but no spoilers until then. Anyways, after that idea had come to fruition (a little bit, at least) the two decided that, if they are going to have an app, there is literally no reason to not start a company. So now, we're here.
